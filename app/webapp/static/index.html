Activity log

What do these mean?

Events — things that happened:

arm / disarm
Alarm armed (full) or disarmed. Source is manual / schedule / presence.
partial / perimeter
Alarm armed in partial or perimeter mode.
ac_lost
Alarm panel lost mains power (running on backup battery) or regained it.
intrusion
Alarm was triggered.
panel_event
An entry from the alarm panel's own event history (RISCO) — the authoritative log, distinct from the live ac_lost/intrusion we detect ourselves.
power_lost / power_restored
The UPS lost or regained mains power.
plug_on / plug_off
A smart plug was switched on or off.

Readings — periodic device measurements (every few minutes):

room_temperature / set_temperature
HVAC measured room temp and its target setpoint (°C).
power_w · voltage_v · current_ma · energy_kwh
Smart-plug live power, voltage, current, and cumulative energy.
battery_charge_pct · runtime_seconds · load_w
UPS battery charge, remaining runtime, and load.
brightness · temperature_k
Light brightness (%) and colour temperature (K).

amber = warning · red = alarm / unreachable. A missing value shows as —.
